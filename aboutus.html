About Us:

Through generations, humans have developed various crafts for cultural expression that shaped and enriched
our lives. We fused the traditional cement tile making technique with the upcycled carbon to handcraft various inspiring patterns.

The recovered carbon collected from multiple sources is analysed and processed. The resulting carbon is then used in making the tiles.
This helps us prevent carbon emissions through building materials. Thus, making Carbon Tile, a commercial solution for Climate Change Action.
